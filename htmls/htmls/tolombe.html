Reserve pump dam

In general, the task of these dams is to supply electricity during peak consumption hours and control electricity during low consumption hours. These types of dams consist of two parts, the upper dam and the lower dam. It is transported and on the way it sets the turbines in motion and electricity is produced, and during low consumption times when there is enough electricity, the water from the lower dam is pumped to the upper dam and stored for times when electricity is needed. Storage pump dams are very economical.
